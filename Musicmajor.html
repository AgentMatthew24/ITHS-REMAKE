Students at ITHS may choose to become “Music Majors” and work towards the New York Commencement Exam in music.  Music Majors complete ten credits of music classes including both ensembles and electives, participate in both solo and ensemble NYSSMA festivals, and complete a final project.  These students become eligible to receive the “John Mondello Music Major Award” upon completion of their high school studies.
